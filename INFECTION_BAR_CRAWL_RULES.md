# ü¶† Infection Bar Crawl Game - Official Rules

## Overview

**Infection Bar Crawl** is a social drinking game where players spread "infection" through the bar by performing challenges, with the goal of either becoming the **Patient Zero** or remaining **Uninfected** the longest.

---

## Setup

### Players & Teams
- **Minimum:** 4 players
- **Ideal:** 8-20 players
- **Recommended:** Split into 2-4 teams, or play free-for-all

### Required Materials
- Red wristbands or markers (for infected players)
- Smartphone or printed challenge cards
- Shot glasses (optional, for penalties)
- A pre-planned bar route (3-5 bars minimum)

### Starting State
- **1 player starts "Infected"** (designated Patient Zero)
- All others are "Uninfected"
- Infected players wear red wristband or mark

---

## Game Mechanics

### How Infection Spreads

An **Infected player** can spread infection by:
1. **Direct Challenge** ‚Äî Challenge an Uninfected player to a game/task
2. **Winning the Challenge** ‚Äî If Infected wins, the Uninfected player becomes Infected
3. **Physical Tag** (Optional) ‚Äî Infected can tag Uninfected to spread infection instantly
4. **Shared Drink** ‚Äî If an Infected player shares a drink with Uninfected, Uninfected becomes Infected

### Infection Immunity

Uninfected players can **avoid infection** by:
- **Winning a challenge** against Infected player
- **Evading tag** (if tag rule is active)
- **Staying isolated** from Infected players
- **Protective shot** ‚Äî Pay $1-2 to the pot for temporary 10-minute immunity

### Challenge Types

**Strength Challenges:**
- Arm wrestling
- Drinking race (beer/shot)
- Push-up/sit-up contest
- Flip cup

**Skill Challenges:**
- Flip a beer cap into a glass (3 tries)
- Pool/darts match
- Karaoke competition (audience votes winner)
- Trivia question

**Social Challenges:**
- Get a stranger's phone number
- Convince bartender to give free shot
- Steal a napkin from another bar
- Find someone wearing same shirt color

**Penalty Challenges** (Infected can impose on Uninfected):
- Finish a drink in 10 seconds
- Sing a song at the bar
- Do 10 pushups
- Buy the Infected player a drink

## Gameplay Rules

### During the Bar Crawl

1. **Duration:** Game runs for entire crawl (typically 4-6 hours, bar-to-bar)
2. **Check-ins:** At each bar, infected/uninfected status is updated
3. **Challenge Frequency:** Average 1 challenge per bar per infected player
4. **Infection Tracking:** Keep a running list of who's infected (write on a napkin or phone note)

### Challenge Resolution

**If Infected challenges Uninfected:**
- Infected proposes challenge
- Challenge happens immediately or at next bar
- **Infected wins** ‚Üí Uninfected becomes Infected (gets wristband)
- **Uninfected wins** ‚Üí Infected becomes Uninfected (loses wristband)

**If Uninfected challenges Infected:**
- Uninfected initiates the challenge
- Same rules apply

### Mandatory Challenges

**Every time a player becomes Infected, they must:**
- Buy the person who infected them a drink, OR
- Complete one "Infection Ritual" (see below)

**Infection Rituals:**
- Announce "I'M INFECTED" loudly at the bar
- Do a shot with the player who infected you
- Wear the wristband visibly for rest of game
- Dance for 30 seconds on or near the dance floor

## Scoring System (Optional)

### Points Awarded

| Achievement | Points |
|-------------|--------|
| Remain Uninfected until final bar | 50 |
| Infect 3+ players in one bar | 25 |
| Win 5 challenges in a row | 30 |
| Get someone else to buy you a drink | 10 |
| Avoid infection for 2 bars after being infected | 15 |
| Become Patient Zero (last Infected standing) | 100 |

### Winning Conditions

**Option 1: Survival**
- Last Uninfected player wins

**Option 2: Dominance**
- Player with most Infected count wins

**Option 3: Highest Score**
- Cumulative point total from challenges

**Option 4: Last Infected**
- Last player still Infected when game ends wins

## Advanced Rules (Optional)

### Mutation
- If 80%+ of players are Infected, remaining Uninfected can "Mutate"
- Mutated players get 2 free challenge wins
- Mutated players wear different color (blue wristband)

### Quarantine
- If a player is infected 3+ times, they enter "Quarantine"
- Quarantined players can't spread infection for 1 bar
- Quarantine is removed after sitting out one bar

### Immunity Powerups
- **Vaccine:** Win 2 challenges = 30-min immunity
- **Mask:** Pay $1 = 15-min immunity (can't share drinks)
- **Sanitizer:** Complete a social challenge = 20-min immunity

### Team Infection
- Infect an entire team = double points
- Teams work together to avoid infection
- If 3+ team members are infected, whole team becomes infected

## Etiquette & Safety

### House Rules
1. **All participants must be 21+** (where applicable) or follow local age restrictions
2. **No drink-spiking** ‚Äî Only shared drinks with consent
3. **No physical harm** ‚Äî Tag is light & playful only
4. **Respect boundaries** ‚Äî If someone says "no challenge," respect it
5. **Buddy system** ‚Äî Everyone must have a friend checking in
6. **Safe transportation** ‚Äî Plan Uber/Lyft/cab before starting
7. **Communicate** ‚Äî Tell bar staff you're playing a game upfront

### Challenge Refusal
- Players can refuse ANY challenge
- Refusing does NOT count as losing
- Refusing a direct infection attempt (tag/shared drink) voids the infection

### Bar Etiquette
- Be respectful to bartenders and other patrons
- Don't block the bar or dance floor
- Pay for your drinks (game shouldn't encourage theft)
- Leave good tips

## Example Game Flow

**Bar 1 (Start)**
- Player A is Patient Zero (Infected)
- A challenges B to arm wrestling ‚Üí A wins ‚Üí B becomes Infected
- B buys A a drink (ritual complete)
- Status: A (I), B (I), C (U), D (U)

**Bar 2**
- B challenges C to trivia ‚Üí C wins ‚Üí B becomes Uninfected
- C challenges D to flip cup ‚Üí C wins ‚Üí D becomes Infected
- D is now Infected, but did infection ritual
- Status: A (I), B (U), C (U), D (I)

**Bar 3**
- A and D both challenge C simultaneously ‚Üí Rock/paper/scissors decides
- A wins ‚Üí C becomes Infected
- Status: A (I), B (U), C (I), D (I)

**Bar 4 (Final)**
- Game ends with A, C, D infected and B uninfected
- **Winner:** B (last Uninfected)
- **Runner-up:** A (infected 2 players: B, C)

## FAQ

**Q: What if everyone is Infected?**
A: Game ends. Last person to become Infected is the Patient Zero (winner). Everyone else loses.

**Q: Can I refuse a challenge?**
A: Yes. You keep your status but lose the chance to win/defend yourself.

**Q: What if two people challenge each other simultaneously?**
A: Play rock/paper/scissors to decide who challenges first, or have them both play the same challenge and both outcomes apply.

**Q: How long should the game last?**
A: 4-6 hours (typical bar crawl duration). Game ends when you leave the last bar or reach a time limit (midnight, 2 AM, etc.).

**Q: Can I go back to being Uninfected after losing to an Infected player?**
A: Only if you win a challenge against someone who is currently Infected.

**Q: What about bathroom breaks?**
A: Bathroom breaks don't reset infection status. You keep whatever status you had.

## Variations

### **Speed Infection Mode**
- Infection spreads faster
- Uninfected get 3-free passes to refuse challenges
- Game ends after 2 hours or 3 bars

### **Team Infection**
- Split into 4-6 team
- Infect entire teams to earn team points
- Team with most infected members at end wins

### **Plague Mode**
- Infected players MUST infect someone each bar
- Failure to infect = become Uninfected
- Last Infected player wins big

### **Silent Infection**
- No verbal challenges allowed
- Infection only spreads through physical tag or shared drink
- Uninfected must actively evade
